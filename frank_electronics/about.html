Our Story

Frank Electronics was founded in Kigali with a simple mission: give every Rwandan access to genuine, high-quality electronics at fair prices — no middlemen, no fakes, no drama.

What started as a small shop in the city centre has grown into one of Rwanda's most trusted tech destinations, serving thousands of happy customers across the country.

We're authorised resellers of Apple, Samsung, Sony, Logitech, Dell, and more — so every product you buy comes with a genuine warranty and full manufacturer support.
